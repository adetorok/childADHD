812P401 Study - SPN-812 Treatment

Supernus Pharmaceuticals

About This Study

This study evaluates SPN-812, an FDA-approved medication for pediatric patients aged 6-17, in preschool-aged children with ADHD.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Between 4 years and 5 years 9 months of age
  • Diagnosed with ADHD
  • Tried non-medication treatment OR symptoms severe enough for medication
  • Attending structured group activity (preschool, kindergarten, sports, etc.)
